In the mood for fish but don’t have much time to cook? Here’s our quick and easy solution: baked prawns – the flavor of the sea in a recipe for an incredibly fragrant fish main course with all the goodness of shellfish and just a few basic ingredients. And that’s not all – we’ve kept mind that some of you will want to keep it light, too, so this is a baked dish that uses a dressing that’s definitely light! Enjoy fresh, meaty, tender prawn with a fresh parsley citronette — perfect for any occasion, at any time of year, combined with your favorite sides, such as an aromatic zucchini salad with mint and basil on a hot summer’s day or a crunchy orange and fennel salad for the winter months. Whatever the weather, these baked prawns are guaranteed to put a smile on the face of whoever tries them!
