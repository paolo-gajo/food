Chocolate profiteroles are a pyramid of goodness! Chef Denny Imbroisi has made this dessert in our kitchens, placing the delicious cream puffs one by one with care and precision! This French dessert with Italian origins has spread all over the world thanks to its utter deliciousness! These morsels of choux pastry are usually filled with Chantilly cream or pastry cream but certainly what characterizes them the most is their chocolate coating! To ensure that it always remains creamy and that the profiteroles are completely coated, you need to pay close attention to the temperature of the icing, this is the only way you will properly coat the profiteroles!
