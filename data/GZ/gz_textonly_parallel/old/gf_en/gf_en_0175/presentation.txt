There are those who prefer it still steaming and those who love it at room temperature, because after all it is well known: the next day it is even better... but parmesan cheese is always parmesan cheese! And if you are used to the more classic version, with eggplant, fried in deep oil, you won't be able to wait to try zucchini parmesan, probably the most famous variation of this fantastic Mediterranean recipe. These authentic tastes offer a unique result that will intoxicate your taste buds. Zucchini parmesan is perfect for any occasion, to be served as an appetizer, a single course or for a summer main dish... one thing is certain, it will win everyone over!
