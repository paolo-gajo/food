What would we do without scalloppini? Delicate sliced veal make even the quickest or most frugal dinners special, as long as you choose the right condiment to tantalize the appetite. We dedicate this recipe to lovers of the fresh and bitter taste of lemon, condensed into a delicious sauce! Lemon scalloppini, just like lemon chicken, will win you over right away, with their delicate and fragrant flavor. We recommend: Generous portions without a doubt, because lemon scalloppini is truly irresistible!
