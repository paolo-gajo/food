Of all the existing desserts, surely the fruit tart is the classic and timeless dessert by right. A fragrant casket of shortcrust pastry that collects a layer of custard and lots of fresh fruit. After all, the always colorful, fresh and fragrant pies are the ideal snack to be enjoyed during the afternoon or at the end of a dinner. For the fruit tart, we always advise you to choose seasonal fruit to decorate and enrich your dessert, creating a personalized tart each time based on the period and your needs. You can also make delicious variations, for example with a base without eggs or in the more original ring shape!
