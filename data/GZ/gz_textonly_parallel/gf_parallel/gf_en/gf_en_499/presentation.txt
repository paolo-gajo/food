For all lovers of roasted chicken, today we offer an irresistible recipe: chicken drumsticks with mustard and bacon. With the addition of mustard brushed directly on the chicken thighs, wrapped with thin slices of bacon, your roasted chicken will become a tasty and juicy dish, appreciated even by mustard skeptics! A dish that contains a skillful mix of flavors and textures: the chicken is softened by mustard and the crispness of bacon makes a tasty contrast. There is no adult or child who can resist this simple goodness! And if you are wondering what the most suitable side dish for chicken drumsticks with mustard and bacon is... well we have no doubt: roasted potatoes!
