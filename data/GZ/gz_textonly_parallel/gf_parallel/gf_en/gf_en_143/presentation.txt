From the finest restaurants to bars on the beach, seafood salad is one of the great classics of the Mediterranean tradition. Its unmistakable flavor, the countless varieties of fish and shellfish that go into it and characterize it, have made it a real institution of Italian cuisine alongside octopus and potato salad. These two tasty dishes are perfect for dinners with friends, Sunday lunch, or as an appetizer during the holidays. Making seafood salad at home with fresh ingredients and a simple parsleyed citronette dressing will be truly satisfying!
