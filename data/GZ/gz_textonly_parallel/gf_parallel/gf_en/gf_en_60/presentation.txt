A classic appetizer: mixed tasty bruschettas with a platter of cold cuts and cheese! It may seem obvious to you to prepare perfect tomato bruschettas, but even this simple recipe has its secrets. In addition to the choice of bread, homemade, preparing the tomatoes seasoned with basil, oil and oregano ahead of time will ensure an even tastier and more fragrant result, a delicious sauce that will further flavor the toasted slices of bread. An extra tasty idea? Just add some mozzarella cubes and a few olives to make a classic caprese bruschetta!
