With the arrival of the hot season we have also made it the protagonist of the summer snack par excellence with avocado and chocolate popsicles, a concentrate of taste and freshness impossible to resist! After seeing how easy it is to make avocado and chocolate popsicles, we bet you will want to try many other tempting flavors such as mango and lime popsicles, golden milk ice cream, yogurt and fruit ice cream on a stick or cream and coffee ice cream. ... one more greedy than the other!
