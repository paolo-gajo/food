To prepare the Negroni cocktail, start by pouring ice into a low tumbler and fill the glass up 3/4 full [1]. Measure the gin [2] and pour [3].
Do the same with the red vermouth by measuring it [4] and pouring [5]. Finally measure and pour the Campari [6].
Stir [7] and garnish with half a slice of orange [8]. Your Negroni cocktail is ready [9]!
