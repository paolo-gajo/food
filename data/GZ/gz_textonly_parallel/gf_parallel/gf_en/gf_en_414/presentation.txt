A good dinner with friends always starts with something tasty. For lovers of fried finger food and appetizing recipes to prepare on short notice, here is an ad hoc solution: fried mozzarella and anchovy skewers. A reinterpretation of the classic mozzarella in carrozza (fried mozzarella), it is transformed into inviting appetizers with double breading to seal the stringy and tasty filling of cheese and anchovies in oil. Find out how easy it is to prepare these delicious skewers and serve them together with other very quick-to-prepare appetizers, such as puff pastry bites or salmon bites. You will make your guests happy!
