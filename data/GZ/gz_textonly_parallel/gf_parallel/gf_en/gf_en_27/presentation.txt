Ever imagined just how creative you can get with a slice of watermelon? Now is the right time to remove it from the refrigerator, freshly bought from the market yesterday, so that together with us you can discover how to make a quick, colorful and fresh watermelon pizza! Don't worry, you won't need to make any dough or meddle with strange ingredient pairings... the base will simply be a juicy and crisp watermelon slice! A surprising and scenic last minute summer dessert, with a creamy mango flavored decoration, garnished with wild berries and edible flowers. For a summer birthday, a beach party or as a dessert to end a summer meal, watermelon pizza is a summer dish that will liven up your holidays! We are sure you will enjoy seconds...and thirds, serving each and every slice of the fruit in this way!
